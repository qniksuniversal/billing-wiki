Chat styles allow you to change the appearance of your in-game chat messages. No impact on gameplay - just beauty.

Players with a **Schalker** subscription have access to all **7 prepared styles**.

### How to choose a style

Open the style selection menu with the command <code>/chat</code> - select the option you like from the available ones. The style is applied only to messages that you see and is not visible to all players in the chat.

![chat_styles](chatstyles/chat1.png)

###Create your own style

**Schalker** subscribers can create a completely **custom chat style**:

1. Go to the server website and open your **profile**.
2. In the block with an active subscription, click the **“Chat Styles”** button.
![chat_styles](chatstyles/chat2.png)
3. Customize the design to your liking.
4. Save - the style will appear in your list of available ones.
