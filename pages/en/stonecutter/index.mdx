## 2. Wood (~221 recipes)

For **each type** of wood, processing recipes for logs, wood, hewn options and boards are added.

### Supported wood types

- 🌳 Oak
- 🌲 Spruce
- 🌿 Birch
- 🌴 Tropical tree
- 🟠Acacia
- 🟤 Dark oak
- 🌊 Mangrove tree
- 🌸 Cherry
- 🫥 Pale Oak
- 🎋 Bamboo
- 🔴 Crimson *(without boat)*
- 🔵 Distorted *(without boat)*
