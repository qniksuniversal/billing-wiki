## Description

You will be able to occupy territories directly in the game. (not to private, but to borrow)
The territory is your busy place where you can set your own rules (except for crazy ones like entry = fine 100 ares, etc.). The occupied territory physically does not provide any restrictions to other players.

**There are restrictions on occupying the territory:**

- From the moment the territory is deleted, 3 days must pass before a new one can be created
