## Interface

The voice chat window can be opened by pressing the <kbd>V</kbd> key. Through it you can:

- Open settings
- Turn off/on the microphone
- Disable voice chat
- Adjust player volume
- Adjust the volume of players

![voice1](voicechat/pv1.png)

## Group chats

Group chats allow you to communicate with players who are not near you.

1. To open the group chat interface, click the group button in the voice chat window or use the key
group chat.

2. To create a new group, enter a name in the text field and click the button below.

![voice2](voicechat/pv2.png)

## Addons

[SoundPhysics](https://modrinth.com/mod/pv-addon-soundphysics)
Adds compatibility with Sound Physics Remastered. Plasmo Voice is influenced by the laws of sound physics.

[Talking Heads](https://modrinth.com/mod/talkingheads)
Client addon for PlasmoVoice and SimpleVoiceChat that changes the size of players' heads during a conversation.
