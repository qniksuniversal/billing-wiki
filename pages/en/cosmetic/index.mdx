Cosmetics are visual effects and decorations that do not affect gameplay, but make your character more noticeable and interesting.

> Cosmetics are available **only for Schalker subscribers**.

### How to open the menu

Open the cosmetics menu with the command <code>/cosmetic</code>.

![Cosmetics menu](cosmetics/cosmetics_beta.png)

### Development status

The cosmetics system is currently being **completely rewritten and redesigned**. Pets and balls are temporarily unavailable - but very soon everything will work in a new form.

Stay tuned.
