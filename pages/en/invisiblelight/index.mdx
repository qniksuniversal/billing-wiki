## Craft

To craft a block of light you need:
- 1 empty bottle
- 1 glowing berry
- 1 glowstone dust
- 1 glowing ink sac

![light1](invisiblelight/light1.png)

## Usage

Each block of light (as a block or as an item) has its own light level, which can range from 0 to 15. In the inventory, the light level of a block of light is displayed in the upper left corner, and the higher the light level, the “brighter” those blocks appear.

When placed as a block, blocks of light are not solid and are completely invisible.

Block lights can be set to the light level in the stone cutter.
