Do you want to have a second account for farming, testing, or just to start from scratch without breaking the rules? This is possible, but only through the official store.

### What is a twink

Twink is a second game account linked to your main one. It allows you to play on the server in parallel or independently, without violating the multi-account rule.

### How to buy a twink

1. Go to the server website and open your **profile**.
2. Find the twink account purchase section.
![twink1](twinks/twink1.png)
3. Purchase a Twink - after this, the second account will be officially linked to your profile.

> A second Discord account is **not needed** for this. Twink is linked to your main account.

### Synchronization of punishments

Twink and main account are connected - all punishments are **synchronized** between them. If a twink gets banned or mutated, the same applies to the main account, and vice versa.

Twink is not a way to get away with punishment.

### Rules for using twink

Using multiple accounts without purchasing a twink is **prohibited** by server rules.

> **Rule 5.2** - Owning a multi-account and circumventing penalties in this way is prohibited.
> The exception is the purchase of a Twink in our store.

Violation of this rule will result in an **IP ban** - blocking by IP address for all accounts.

### Briefly about the main thing

- Creating a second account yourself is **prohibited**.
- Buying a Twink in a store is **allowed**.
- A second Discord is not needed.
- For bypassing through a multi-account - **IP-ban**.
