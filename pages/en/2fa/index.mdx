## Licensed

If you have a licensed version of the game, then you do not need to enable 2FA, but you will need to confirm your license. To do this, enter the command `/premium`. After confirming your licensed account, you will not need to enter a password, and players from the pirated version of the game will not be able to log in under your nickname, since it is not licensed. [Connection guide Premium](/wiki/account-premium)

P.S. If you are afraid that your license may be stolen, then you can connect 2FA with your license account.

## No license

The security of your account is always above all else, because after hacking it will be difficult for you to prove to the project administration that you were hacked.

### 2FA connection instructions

1. Join the Schalker Vanilla server and enter the command `/addsocial`.
![2fa1](2fa/2fa1.png)
2. Choose your favorite social network. In my example, we connect the discord account with the command `!account link username` (Write the command in private messages on one of the social networks).
![2fa2](2fa/2fa2.png)
3. The bot will answer you and ask you to write the command `/addsocial code` (Write to the Minecraft server).
![2fa3](2fa/2fa3.png)
4. Congratulations! You have connected your social network, now you can manage your account and make logins/exits to your account.

### How to confirm login to the server?

After logging into the server, you enter the password for your account as usual. After entering, the server will ask you to confirm your entry.
![2fa4](2fa/2fa4.png)

You will receive a message on the linked social network asking you to confirm your login.
![2fa5](2fa/2fa5.png)

The "It's not me" button kicks the player from the server.
![2fa6](2fa/2fa6.png)

### Other features with linked social network
"Information" button - shows mini-information about the player.
![2fa7](2fa/2fa7.png)

Button "Ban/Unban" - blocks/unblocks your account. (This is not an account ban, but only a blocking of login to the server)

Button "2FA On/Off" - turns on/off login confirmation.

Button "Notifications On/Off" - turns on/off notifications about entering the server.

"Kick" button - kicks you from the server.

"Restore" button - changes your password.

"Disconnect" button - disconnects you from the social network.
