Helmet? What a helmet. A real player wears what he wants on his head. Cactus, pumpkin, chest, oak block are all legal headgear on Schalker Vanilla.

### How to put an item on your head

Take the desired item in your hand and enter the command <code>/hat</code>. The item will move directly onto your character's head.

All. Nothing more is needed. No logic, no restrictions - just you and your hat.

![hat](hat/hat.png)

### What to wear

Almost anything. Here are some proven options for inspiration:

- **Watermelon** is a classic of the genre. It's difficult to breathe, but stylish.
- **Chest** - reliably hides the face and contents at the same time.
- **Cactus** - not recommended by anyone except those who have already tried it.
- **Cake** - why not.

### Practical benefits

She's gone. But this was never a requirement.

The item on the head does not protect, does not warm or feed. But it gives +100 to individuality - and this is more expensive than any armor.
