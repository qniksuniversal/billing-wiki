Sometimes words are not enough. Sometimes you just have to cough it up.

### How to spit

Write the command <code>/spit</code> - and you're done. Your character will express everything that is boiling, without a single word.

### Important clarifications

- Spitting does not cause damage. Unfortunately.
- You can spit as much as you like - no cooldowns, no morals.
- The surrounding players see everything. This is part of the plan.

Use wisely. Or don't use it wisely. I'm not a judge, I'm a combogames guy.
