Tired of traveling through harsh biomes alone? Do you want to share with someone the joy of finding a diamond or the grief of dying in lava? Then it's time to get married.

March 8 at Schalker Vanilla was held as usual in Schalker style - da nu naxui blyat kaak vsegda shashlichnaya vanilla.

![wedding](weddings/wedding.png)

### How to get married

Just writing a command in the chat is not enough - marriages are registered by a **priest**. Find one of them, agree on the ceremony and come with your chosen one.

1. Find a priest - a player with the appropriate role.
2. The priest conducts the ceremony with the command <code>/marry `ник1` `ник2`</code>.
3. Both players will receive a confirmation request.
4. Everyone must write <code>/marry confirm</code> to agree.
5. If someone changes their mind, <code>/marry deny</code> will reject the offer.

> A priest is not just a formality. There won’t be any wedding without him, even if you shout into the chat as much as you like.

### Marriage Information

Want to know who is married to whom? Or check to see if your union is still alive?

- <code>/marry info</code> - your personal marital status card.
- <code>/marry info `ник`</code> - marital status of the other player.
- <code>/marry list</code> - a complete list of all pairs on the server.
