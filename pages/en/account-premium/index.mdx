### Enable licensed login type.
Players with a licensed account can log into the server without a password.

1. Go to the server, log into your account as usual.
2. Write the command `/premium`
![prem1](account-premium/prem1.png)
3. Click on the text `Нажмите для подтверждения` in the chat, then re-login to the server.
![prem2](account-premium/prem2.png)
4. Done! If the players' heads are shown in the tab, then everything works.
![prem3](account-premium/Premium.png)

### Disable licensed login type.
1. Go to the server, log into your account as usual.
2. Write the command `/premium`
3. Click on the text `Нажмите для подтверждения` in the chat, then re-login to the server.
4. Done!
