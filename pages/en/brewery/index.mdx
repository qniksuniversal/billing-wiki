### Usage
Unlike vanilla Minecraft, it's not as simple as adding an ingredient to the brewery and waiting for it to run out. Depending on the recipe, the entire process can be complex and time-consuming. Some recipes may require great precision when it comes to ingredients or any other step in the process. If one step was not good enough, the quality of the potions may suffer, leading to side effects such as bad alcohol and other toxins.
This new difficulty in preparing a good drink only makes sense if players do not know the exact recipes for at least more valuable potions (for example, rum). This adds value to high-quality potions, as well as encourages experimentation and improvement in the brewing process.
Depending on the recipe, some steps may not be necessary; the following instructions describe the general brewing procedure.

### Fermentation
The first step is to ferment the fresh ingredients.
1. Place the boiler on the fire or other heat source
2. Fill it with water
3. Add ingredients by right clicking
4. Wait a few minutes for them to wander.
5. Fill glass bottles

![Boiler](brewery/brew1.png)

### Distillation
Some recipes do not require distillation.
1. Place the bottle with the starter in the brewing rack
2. Place glowstone dust as a filter on top of the brewing stand (the filter will not be used up)

![Potion maker](brewery/brew2.png)

### Excerpt
Small barrel - 9 slots
Use 8 wooden ladders to create a barrel shape.
Place the Sign in the lower right corner and write "Barrel" on the top line:

![Small barrel](brewery/brew3.png)

Large barrel - 27 slots
Use 5 fences, 16 wooden ladders and 18 wooden planks to create a barrel shape.
Attach the tap (guard) and the sign that says "Barrel":

![Big barrel](brewery/brew4.png)

<strong>To remove the barrel, simply break the sign.</strong>
Open the barrel by clicking on it.
1. Place bottles in Aging Barrels.
2. For every day of Minecraft, the beer will be aged for a year.
3. Depending on the recipe, the type of wood used to make the barrel can affect the quality of the aged beer.
4. A barrel for Minecraft is always made of wood such as oak.

### Drink

The amount of alcohol will be applied to the player when drinking. Depending on the quality, this can have different consequences.
1. The player may no longer be able to walk normally, they will waddle, making it almost impossible to walk straight
2. Effects such as blindness, confusion, poisoning, etc. occur.
3. Chat will be changed depending on the level of intoxication, many things that players write may not be clear, sometimes it seems pointless
4. Alcohol is especially strong and can be toxic.

### How to get sober
After consumption, it takes some time until the alcohol completely disappears. During this time, alcohol levels drop steadily. You can also drink a lot of milk or eat a lot of bread and you will sober up faster.
