## Usage

The bag can store a total of only one stack of items (for example, only 16 eggs or only 64 diamonds) or a total of 16 or 64 different items if there are no full stacks of one of the items in the bag (for example: 32 gold bars and 32 iron bars; 2 gold bars, 5 iron bars and 57 copper bars; 8 eggs and 2 iron bars).

If an item does not stack (for example, a sword or a pickaxe), then only that item will fit into the bag; nothing else can be placed with it, since it will be counted as a stack of items.

Can be placed in a shulker box, but not in another bag.

To put items in a bag, you need to open your inventory, take the bag and click on the desired item/s (by default <kbd>RMB</kbd>). You can remove all items from the bag by pressing <kbd>RMB</kbd> on the bag while holding it in your hand. To remove one item, you need to click <kbd>RMB</kbd> on the bag in your inventory. If the bag is not completely filled, it will display a fullness scale showing the approximate occupied volume. When you hover your cursor over a bag, a list and the number of items in the bag are displayed.
