Kertix is a universal hosting provider offering digital solutions for clients of different sizes. We work worldwide and use this wiki to collect answers, instructions, and larger guides for installing and configuring software.

## Why Kertix

We build hosting that is convenient to use every day: from game servers and websites to VDS, databases, backups, and additional services.

### Performance

- virtual services run on **AMD Ryzen 9 9950X3D**;
- fast **NVMe drives** with speeds up to **7000 MB/s**;
- servers are connected at up to **2 Gbit/s**;
- your plan resources can be loaded at **100%**.

We do not use swap on your services and do not sell it as a separate paid option.

### Protection

All servers include basic **L3/L4** DDoS protection. You can also add **L7 protection** for VDS: it works on all ports and helps protect a website, game project, and other services at once.

## Convenient control panel

The Kertix panel is built so common actions take as little time as possible.

- different navigation styles;
- multiple interface languages;
- one-click mod and plugin installation through Modrinth repositories;
- quick server, plan, firewall, and load monitoring controls;
- ready-made website and forum templates;
- separate ready-made MTA builds.

For VDS, you can reinstall the server, request a plan upgrade, configure firewall rules, or monitor load in just a couple of clicks.

## Additional services

We offer more than basic hosting. Depending on your task, you can connect:

- a custom port;
- additional backups;
- databases;
- S3 storage;
- a convenient proxy for hiding the port of a dynamic map;
- other services that can be bought in one click or requested through a ticket.

## Payments and infrastructure

We pay taxes, accept payments in almost all world currencies, and work with cryptocurrency.

All servers are hosted in our own mini data center. This helps us control infrastructure, maintenance, and service quality.

## Support

Our support team helps with Minecraft, MTA, websites, VDS, and other services. If you do not know how to configure something or run into an error, create a ticket.

Support works every day from **8:00 to 21:00** in your time zone.

## Tests and free servers

Need a test before paying? You can request a **72-hour** test for any plan. A new test is available once every **10 days**.

For partners and large events, we can provide a server for a few days for free. If you are planning an event for a large audience, contact support.

## Still have questions?

If you still have doubts or want to clarify details, contact technical support. We will explain, show, and help you choose the right solution.
