### How to start playing on the Schalker Vanilla project?

Schalker - This is a private, vanilla server created for intimate and close-knit games of people. The server does not have private territories, unnecessary plugins or privileges. You can access the server from the pirated and licensed versions of the game.

Before you start, be sure to read the Rules and Information in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/schalker)

### First, where to start?

1. Join the server [Discord](https://discord.gg/schalker)
2. Fill out the application and wait for approval.

![Request](start/Zayavka.png)

3. After acceptance, being added to the whitelist occurs automatically, you will be able to log in immediately!

### Second - how to log in?

1. Connect to the server using the version specified in the Information channel

![Server ping](start/ServerPing.png)

2. Register or log in to your account

![Registration](start/startreg.png)

3. You will be transferred to the lobby of our server
4. Jump into the portal above which there is the inscription Schalker Vanilla.

![Lobby](start/lobbynew.png)

5. You will be asked to link your discord account to the site (it’s not difficult)
6. When you click, they will send you a link in the chat. You need to click on it and follow the link, log in via discord and your profile will be automatically created.

![Bot](start/linkingcode.png)

![Enter code](start/siteactivate.png)

7. If the site responded that “Your Minecraft account is linked to Discord and the site. Now you can use all the functions of the site.” then you did everything right, congratulations!

### Enjoy your game!
